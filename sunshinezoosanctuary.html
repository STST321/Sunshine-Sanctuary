Come say hello to all our animals, they're looking forward to meeting you!

All the animals in our zoo sanctuary are rescues who are now safe and happy living in our sanctuary. Mr Piggles the pot-bellied pig, Dora the miniature donkey and Bugsey the giant bunny are some of the latest additions to our family. Help us make them feel welcomed by treating them to some tasty carrots from our farm shop, and give them a much-loved pet! You can also sponsor any of the animals to help with their care, ask our zoo helpers for more information.
