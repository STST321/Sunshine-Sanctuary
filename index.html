About Us

Welcome to our sanctuary where there is so much to do! Browse our farm shop and plant nursery stocked with locally grown produce, visit our rescue animals, have fun at the play park or go for a walk in the nearby ancient woodlands, then relax with a delicious meal in our beautiful farmhouse cafe!
