Let your inner kid have fun, or zen out in one of our woodland walks...

Our play park is designed for adults and children to enjoy, from the monkey bars to the swings and see-saw, its a wonderful opportunity for the whole family to connect and have heaps of fun! If you are looking to escape into nature, we offer guided walks through the ancient woodlands that surround the centre. Learn about woodland biodiversity and do a bit of wild foraging with our experts!
